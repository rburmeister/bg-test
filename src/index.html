Transforming Healthcare through Innovation and Advanced Manufacturing

The VHA Office of Advanced Manufacturing envisions a future where eligible Veterans receive personalized, safe, and equitable care through cutting-edge manufacturing capabilities.

Vision

The vision of the VHA Office of Advanced Manufacturing is to provide personalized, safe and equitable care for eligible Veterans by building advanced manufacturing capabilities across the enterprise.

Assistive Technology Catalog

VHA is partnering with the National Institutes of Health (NIH) to bring you the Assistive Technology (AT) Catalog, a collection of AT devices that are products of collaboration between patients, therapists, and engineers to match a patient’s needs and abilities with customized technology that helps them achieve their goals. This collection aims to share AT designs from the Central Virginia VA Health Care System and derivatives from users, along with stories and print information from VHA and the National Institutes of Health communities. The collection is scheduled to launch in early 2024. For more information or to request these files in the interim, please contact john.miller@va.gov.
